FAQ

Questions? We have answers.

Still need help? Reach out to our team at support@flowdesk.ai

Can I change plans later?
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle, and any prorated amount is automatically applied to your balance.
Is there a free trial?
All plans include a 14-day free trial with full access to the features of your selected tier. No credit card is required to get started. At the end of the trial, you can subscribe or let it expire — no automatic charges.
Do I need technical knowledge to use FlowDesk AI?
Not at all for the Starter and Pro plans. The visual workflow builder is designed to be intuitive for non-technical users. Enterprise customers who need custom workflow architecture will receive guided onboarding from our implementation team.
What happens if I cancel?
If you cancel, your account remains active until the end of the current billing period. After that, your workspace is locked but not deleted — you have 30 days to export your data before it is permanently removed.
Do you offer enterprise onboarding?
Yes. Enterprise customers receive a dedicated success manager who leads the full onboarding process, including workflow architecture design, team training, and integration setup. Contact our sales team to learn more.
